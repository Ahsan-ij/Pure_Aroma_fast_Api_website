Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients are used in your fragrances?

We use a blend of natural and synthetic ingredients to create unique scents while ensuring safety and quality.

How should I store my fragrance?

To maintain the quality of your fragrance, store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Can I return a fragrance if I don't like it?

Yes, we offer a return policy for unopened items within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our returns page for more details.

How do I choose the right fragrance for me?

Consider the notes that appeal to you, and explore our collection. You can also visit our store for personalized recommendations.

Do you offer samples of your fragrances?

Yes, we offer sample sizes for most of our fragrances so you can find your perfect scent before committing to a full bottle.
